# Key priors

- **Hu et al. 2019, Joint Monocular 3D Vehicle Detection and Tracking:** useful monocular 3D baseline; not planner-utility focused.
- **Bartl et al. 2018, 3D Vehicle Trajectory Reconstruction in Monocular Video Data Using Environment Structure Constraints:** relevant for constrained recovery assumptions.
- **Caesar et al. 2022, nuPlan:** core justification for closed-loop rather than open-loop evaluation.
- **Ettinger et al. 2021, Waymo Open Motion Dataset:** strong support for interaction-rich trajectory evaluation.
- **Zhan et al., INTERACTION dataset:** useful source for negotiation-heavy scenario analysis.

## Durable lesson
The gap is the bridge between monocular reconstruction metrics and downstream planner-facing validity.
