# Citation / faithfulness line (FactCC, citation-aware summarization)

## Relevance
Important for evaluating whether compressed summaries preserve answer-critical facts and remain auditable.

## Durable takeaway
Faithfulness is part of the causal test, not a side metric. Summary retrieval can look strong on QA while still dropping or distorting decisive evidence.

## Limitation for this project
Current draft evidence suggests citation-heavy intermediate summaries may cost too much budget and may be ignored by small models; treat citation as optional until a tiny matched-budget pilot shows real downstream use.
